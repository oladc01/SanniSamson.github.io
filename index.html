Mediterranean Avocado Salad

A vibrant and refreshing Mediterranean Avocado Salad brimming with colors and flavor. This wholesome dish features creamy avocado chunks, juicy cherry tomatoes, crisp arugula and mixed greens, and briny black olives. It’s sprinkled with crumbled feta cheese and lightly dressed in a zesty olive oil vinaigrette. The salad is paired with a slice of rustic whole grain bread, perfect for scooping up the flavorful mix. Light, nutritious, and bursting with M editerranean charm—this is the perfect balance of fresh and savory in every bite.

Banana Nut Power Bowl

Start your day strong with this hearty Banana Nut Power Bowl, a wholesome blend of textures and natural sweetness. Creamy oatmeal forms the warm base, topped with fresh banana slices, crunchy granola, and a generous spoonful of peanut butter. A splash of almond milk adds a smooth finish, while a mix of nuts and seeds—like almonds and flaxseeds—offers a satisfying crunch and a protein boost. Perfect for breakfast or a post-workout refuel, this nourishing bowl is both comforting and energizing.

Green Detox Bowl

Clean, crisp, and nutrient-dense, the Green Detox Bowl is a celebration of vibrant, fresh vegetables. This bowl is packed with powerhouse greens like broccoli florets, kale, spinach, asparagus spears, and a crunchy green bell pepper. At the heart lies a perfectly ripe avocado, adding creamy richness and healthy fats. Ideal for a detox meal or a nourishing plant-based boost, this bowl is a fiber-rich, vitamin-packed choice that supports energy, digestion, and overall well-being—pure green goodness in every bite.

Mediterranean Quinoa Meal Prep

The Mediterranean Quinoa Meal Prep is a wholesome and convenient option designed for health-conscious individuals on the go. This beautifully layered dish features a hearty base of nutty quinoa, topped with a vibrant mix of sweet corn kernels, juicy cherry tomatoes, crisp cucumber slices, and briny black olives. Fresh green onions and lentils add a satisfying texture and protein boost, while a sprinkle of herbs enhances the Mediterranean flair. Packaged in convenient glass containers, this meal is perfect for a nutritious lunch or dinner, offering a balanced blend of flavors and nutrients.

Price:# 3,100

Veggie Delight snack Platter

Food Description: The Veggie Delight Snack Platter is a fresh and vibrant assortment perfect for a light meal or a healthy snack. This platter features an array of crisp, colorful vegetables, including cucumber slices, cherry tomatoes, carrot sticks, and broccoli florets, all arranged for easy dipping. At the center, a creamy beetroot hummus takes the spotlight, garnished with chia seeds and a drizzle of olive oil, offering a tangy and earthy flavor that pairs beautifully with the veggies. Accompanying the hummus is a bowl of briny black olives, adding a savory Mediterranean touch. The platter is completed with a stack of whole-grain crackers, providing a satisfying crunch to round out this wholesome and flavorful snack option.

Price:# 3,500

Tropical Berry Bliss Bowl

The Tropical Berry Bliss Bowl is a vibrant and nutritious breakfast or snack option that bursts with flavor and color. This delightful bowl features a rich, creamy purple base made from blended berries and yogurt, topped with a generous sprinkle of shredded coconut and chia seeds for added texture and health benefits. It’s adorned with an assortment of fresh fruits, including juicy strawberries, sweet mango slices, tangy kiwi, plump blueberries, and tart blackberries, creating a tropical paradise in every bite. Garnished with a sprig of fresh parsley and served alongside golden muffins, this bowl is a perfect harmony of sweet and tangy flavors, ideal for a refreshing start to the day.
